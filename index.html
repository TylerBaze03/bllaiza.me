    Babel

    How is a story that good? Arknights story always been incredibly laid out and just peak from top to bottom, but how do you tie just about evertyhing from basically the start of the game to the into the neat yarn ball that is Babel? So many answers along with just about as many questions to replace the old ones, also extremely exciting to see where Arknights:Endfield takes the story; I can only imagine it just builds on this as the most fascinating part of it is that in Endfield Oripathy is cured, or as they put it no longer an issue. This can both be conforting and worrying but especially with how the last couple of chapters of Babel were laid out. I wonder what this means for the doctor and what his original plan was regarding everything he did in the event. I really havent been this engrossed in a story since FFXIV and I'm very happy to have that same spark again for wanting to learn everything about the world and intracacies of the world given to us by Hypergrph. Endfield I assume will give many answers as well as the resolution of the Victoria arc, which I assume is reaching its penultimate number.

    It always baffles me how people think gacha stories are useless, this is possibly one of the best stories I have ever readl, which is why I will never stop playing and enjoying live service games. :p
